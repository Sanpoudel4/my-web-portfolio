My Journey

My journey into web development began with a deep fascination for how design and technology work together to shape the way people interact with the digital world. I started with the essentials such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript and quickly discovered how even simple lines of code could create functional and elegant experiences.

Over time, I expanded my skills into modern frameworks and responsive design systems, mastering tools like React, Tailwind CSS, and PHP. My studies at NAIT in the Digital Media & IT – Web Design & Development program helped me combine creativity with technical precision and guided me toward becoming a detail-driven front-end developer.

Beyond code, I have learned the value of collaboration and human-centered design. Working on real client projects and team-based assignments taught me to balance usability, accessibility, and performance while creating experiences that are both beautiful and intuitive. Every project challenges me to think more critically, design more creatively, and code more efficiently.

Today, my goal is to keep learning, experimenting, and evolving to build digital experiences that connect people, tell stories, and make technology feel effortless. My journey is not just about building websites. It is about crafting meaningful digital experiences.
